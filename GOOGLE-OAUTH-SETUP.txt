TROUBLESHOOTING
═════════════════

Error: "redirect_uri_mismatch"
Fix: Add exact redirect URL in Google Console:
     https://gsqpobjgxrkeyxfqlakb.supabase.co/auth/v1/callback

Error: "access_denied"
Fix: Add your email to test users in OAuth consent screen

Error: "invalid_client"
Fix: Check Client ID and Secret are correct in Supabase

Error: "This app isn't verified"
Fix: Click "Advanced" → "Go to FutureTech (unsafe)"
     (This is normal for development)
